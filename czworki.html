DZIELONE NA CZTERY or /4 was a personal brand project created by myself and a couple of other web developers, designers and copywriters. The idea behind it was to use the brand as a sort of collaboration network for our freelance projects - if a project's area of expertise was reaching beyond one of ours' capabilities, we would reach out to other members of the group for collaboration and sign our work with the /4 mark, signifying the initial number of the group's members.

Well, that, and we wanted to have cooler business cards.
